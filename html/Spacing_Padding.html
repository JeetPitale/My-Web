Cell Padding

Cell padding is the amount of space in pixels between the edges of the cell and cell content. You can specify the n cell padding with the cellpadding attribute of the <table> tag.

Example :

<!DOCTYPE html>
<html>
<head>
<title>Cell Padding </title>
</head>
<body>
<table height="200" width="500" style="padding: 10px;" border="3">
<tr>
<th>First Name</th>
<th>Last Name</th>
<th>Points</th>
</tr>
<tr>
<td>Peter</td>
<td>Griffin</td>
<td>$100</td>
</tr>
<tr>
<td>Lois</td>
<td>Griffin</td>
<td>$150</td>
</tr>
<tr>
<td>Joe</td>
<td>Swanson</td>
<td>$300</td>
</tr>
<tr>
<td>Cleveland</td>
<td>Brown</td>
<td>$250</td>
</tr>
</table>
</body>
</html>

Cell Spacing

Cell cellspacing spacing is the amount of the space in pixels between the cell borders. You can specify the cell spacing with the cellspacing attribute of the <table> tag.

Example :

<!DOCTYPE html>
<html>
<head>
<title>Cell Padding and Cell Spacing</title>
</head>
